About Us

Welcome to CoastCrave, where every meal is a splash of happiness! Established in 2023, we aim to bring the taste of the ocean closer to you through fast, easy, and reliable online ordering. We believe everyone deserves freshly prepared seafood anytime, anywhere, made with care, passion, and a promise of quality. Our mission is to be the leading online platform for fresh and flavorful seafood, trusted by customers for quality, convenience, and excellent service, while our vision is to provide high-quality seafood dishes through a fast, reliable, and user-friendly online ordering experience. At CoastCrave, we don't just serve seafood we serve smiles, comfort, and satisfaction in every bite, so order now and taste the freshness you deserve!

Find Us

Our Location in San Pascual

Located in San Pascual, Batangas, close to well-known seafood markets in nearby towns like Bauan and Batangas City. We source our seafood from trusted suppliers to ensure consistent freshness and quality.

Travel Guide

Getting Here

We're conveniently located in San Pascual, Batangas. You can reach us by private vehicle or public transportation from Manila in approximately 2-3 hours.
